Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna is a comforting dish with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese

  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
