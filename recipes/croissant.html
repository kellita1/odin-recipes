Croissant Recipe

Description

These buttery, flaky croissants are a classic French pastry that's perfect for breakfast or a special treat. Their delicate layers make every bite irresistible.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups cold unsalted butter (for layering)

Directions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, yeast, warm milk, and melted butter. Knead the dough until smooth, then let it rest and rise for about 1 hour.
  2. Roll the dough into a large rectangle, place the cold butter in the center, and fold the dough over it. Roll it out and fold multiple times to form thin layers.
  3. Cut and shape the dough into croissants. Brush with egg wash, then bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15–20 minutes until golden and flaky.
