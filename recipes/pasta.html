Pasta Alfredo Recipe

Description

This classic Pasta Alfredo is rich, creamy, and incredibly easy to make. With just a few ingredients—cream, butter, and Parmesan cheese—you can whip up a restaurant-style dinner in under 30 minutes.

Perfect for busy weeknights or elegant dinners, this dish is comforting and satisfying. Customize it with grilled chicken, shrimp, or vegetables if you'd like, or enjoy it in its simple, delicious form.

Ingredients

  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic if using and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer gently for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Lower the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese. Mix until the sauce is smooth.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat. Use reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
